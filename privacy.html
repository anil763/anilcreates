Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Anil Creates LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website anilcreatesugc.com or engage with our services.

1. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: First name, last name, email address, phone number, and company name when you submit our contact form or otherwise communicate with us.
  • Mobile Phone Number: When you voluntarily provide your phone number and opt in to receive SMS/text messages from us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our website, including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited, time and date of visits, time spent on pages, and referring website addresses.
  • Communication Records: Records of correspondence if you contact us, including via email, phone, or SMS text messages.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our website, including hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our content creation services
  • To send transactional communications related to your project or inquiry
  • To send SMS/text messages related to your inquiry, project updates, appointment reminders, and service notifications (with your explicit opt-in consent)
  • To send marketing and promotional text messages about our services and offers (only with your separate, explicit opt-in consent)
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience
  • To analyze website usage and trends
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. SMS/Text Message Communications

By opting in to receive text messages from Anil Creates LLC, you expressly consent to receive SMS/MMS messages from us at the phone number you provided.

Details of our SMS communications program:

  • Types of Messages: You may receive messages related to your inquiry, project status updates, appointment reminders, and — if you separately opt in — marketing promotions and special offers.
  • Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your engagement and project activity. Transactional messages are sent as needed. Marketing messages are limited to no more than 4 messages per month.
  • Message and Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Anil Creates LLC is not responsible for any charges incurred from your carrier.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and will no longer receive text messages from us.
  • Help: For help or questions about our SMS program, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at anil@anilcreates.com.
  • Consent Not Required for Purchase: Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services from Anil Creates LLC.

4. Mobile Information Sharing — No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, share, or otherwise disclose your mobile phone number or any personal information collected through our SMS program to any third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

Your phone number and SMS opt-in data will only be used by Anil Creates LLC to communicate with you as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share your phone number with service providers (such as our SMS delivery platform) solely for the purpose of delivering messages to you on our behalf, and these providers are contractually prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

5. Cookies & Tracking Practices

Our website may use the following cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality such as page navigation and form submission.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use services like Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information anonymously, including the number of visitors, pages visited, and traffic sources.
  • Third-Party Pixels: We may use tracking pixels from advertising platforms (e.g., Meta, Google Ads) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, delete existing cookies, and set preferences for certain websites.

6. Data Security and Handling

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit via SSL/TLS (HTTPS)
  • Access controls limiting who within our organization can view personal information
  • Secure storage of data through trusted third-party service providers
  • Regular review of our data collection, storage, and processing practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit how we use your personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out of SMS: You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at anil@anilcreates.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our business (e.g., email platforms, CRM software, SMS delivery services), subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
